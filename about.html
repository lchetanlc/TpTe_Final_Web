What is TPTE ?

Today's Programmer Tomorrow's Engineer (TPTE) is a certified student organization of Lovely Professional University.

TPTE comes under the 'Academic and Technical' category.The main motive of our organization is to provide assistance to the students by organising various events and workshops. We as a team try our level best to deliver the desired goals of the students.

Why is TPTE ?

We exist for the purpose of bringing students together, helping them acclimate to life at the university, connecting them to other students, as well as alumni and can help students develop transferable skills.

In TpTe, students can enhance their skills by involving in various activities conducted by the organization in their respective domains.Through organizations, students can meet with each others, grow as individuals , and help find their place at school. It’s also a great way for students to relax and have fun with their peers.
